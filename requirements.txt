GRAB & GO: LOOT ON THE RUN - REQUIREMENTS
===========================================

PROJECT INFORMATION
-------------------
Project Name: Grab & Go: Loot on the Run
Theme: "You are not the main character"
Developer: Harriet Maleck (ST10252836)
Development Period: March 24-26, 2026
Platform: Windows PC
Engine: Unity 2022.3+


FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
-----------------------

FR1: PLAYER CONTROL SYSTEM
- The player shall control a support character (getaway partner), not the thief
- Player movement shall be limited to 4 discrete lanes
- Input shall support keyboard (A/D keys and Arrow keys)
- Lane switching shall be instantaneous with smooth position transitions
- Player character shall move forward automatically at constant speed

FR2: AI THIEF BEHAVIOR
- The thief character shall run forward autonomously
- The thief shall avoid obstacles using simple rule-based AI
- The thief shall make lane-switching decisions independently
- The thief shall maintain a configurable distance ahead of the player
- The thief shall have a "caught" state when hitting obstacles

FR3: COLLECTIBLE SYSTEM
- The thief shall randomly drop collectible items during gameplay
- Collectibles shall include multiple types with different values:
  * Coins: Low value (1 point)
  * Gold bars: High value (5 points)
- Drop rates shall be weighted and configurable
- Collectibles shall only be picked up by the player character
- Collection shall be immediate upon player contact
- Each collectible shall have an associated audio cue
- Coins shall rotate continuously for visual distinction
- Gold bars shall remain static (no rotation)

FR4: OBSTACLE SYSTEM
- Obstacles shall spawn randomly in lanes ahead of player
- Obstacles shall include police vehicles (cars, buses)
- Player collision with obstacles shall reduce health by configurable amount
- Obstacles shall despawn after collision or when behind player
- Obstacles shall use proper trigger colliders for detection
- AI thief shall attempt emergency lane changes when approaching obstacles

FR5: TIMER SYSTEM
- A countdown timer shall represent police arrival/escape time
- Default timer duration: 120 seconds (2 minutes)
- Timer shall count down in real-time during gameplay
- Timer shall display in MM:SS format
- Timer color shall change based on remaining time:
  * Cyan (normal): >30% remaining
  * Orange (warning): 10-30% remaining
  * Red (danger): <10% remaining
- Timer shall trigger end condition when reaching 0:00

FR6: HEALTH SYSTEM
- Player shall have maximum health value (default: 100)
- Health shall decrease when hitting obstacles
- Health shall be displayed as slider bar and numeric value
- Health damage per obstacle: 10 points (configurable)

FR7: AUDIO SYSTEM
- Game shall support two audio channels:
  * Ambient: Continuous city soundscape
  * Gameplay: Event-triggered sounds (pickups, collisions)
- Ambient sounds shall play immediately on game start
- Ambient playback shall be continuous with no gaps
- Gameplay sounds shall have randomized intervals
- Audio clips shall not repeat consecutively
- Volume and pitch shall have random variation

FR8: UI/HUD SYSTEM
- HUD shall display 6 core elements simultaneously:
  1. Escape timer (top center)
  2. Thief status (top left)
  3. Loot counter (top right)
  4. Health bar (bottom left)
  5. Alert feed (bottom center)
  6. Controls hint (bottom right)
- All UI elements shall update in real-time
- Alert messages shall auto-hide after 0.5 seconds
- Controls hint shall auto-hide after 10 seconds

FR9: GAME FLOW CONTROL
- Game shall start with menu screen displayed
- Gameplay shall be paused until play button is clicked
- Menu panel shall hide when gameplay begins
- HUD panel shall be visible only during active gameplay
- Gameplay components shall be disabled until game starts

FR10: SCENE MANAGEMENT
- Road tiles shall spawn ahead of player continuously
- Road tiles shall despawn when behind player by threshold distance
- Collectibles shall be parented to road tiles for automatic cleanup
- Scene shall maintain stable active object count via despawning


NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------

NFR1: PERFORMANCE
- Game shall maintain 60 FPS on mid-range PC hardware
- Maximum active obstacles: 40 simultaneous objects
- Despawn distance: 120 units behind player
- No memory leaks from spawning/despawning objects

NFR2: RESPONSIVENESS
- Player input shall have <16ms latency (one frame at 60 FPS)
- Collectible pickup shall feel immediate (no visible delay)
- UI updates shall occur within same frame as event
- Lane switching shall complete within 0.2 seconds

NFR3: USABILITY
- Controls shall be intuitive and explained to player
- Visual feedback for all player actions (pickups, collisions)
- Audio feedback for all significant events
- Color-coded UI elements for quick readability
- No hidden mechanics or unclear objectives

NFR4: MAINTAINABILITY
- Code shall use single-responsibility components
- Inspector-exposed variables for all tunable values
- Centralized utilities for shared calculations (LaneMath)
- Clear naming conventions throughout codebase
- Comments for non-obvious implementation details

NFR5: SCALABILITY
- System shall support adding new collectible types easily
- Drop table shall be data-driven via Inspector
- Audio system shall scale with additional clips
- Lane count shall be configurable (currently 4)

NFR6: RELIABILITY
- No null reference exceptions during normal gameplay
- Graceful handling of missing Inspector references
- Proper initialization order via Awake/Start pattern
- Defensive programming for edge cases

NFR7: COMPATIBILITY
- Project shall build successfully for Windows platform
- UI shall scale properly across different resolutions
- Audio shall work with stereo and mono output devices
- No platform-specific dependencies


DESIGN CONSTRAINTS
------------------

DC1: THEME ALIGNMENT
- Player must NOT directly control the thief (main character)
- AI thief must make autonomous decisions
- Player role limited to support actions only

DC2: TECHNICAL CONSTRAINTS
- Unity 2022.3+ required
- Built-in Render Pipeline (no URP/HDRP dependency)
- Standard physics system (no custom physics)
- TextMesh Pro for UI text rendering

DC3: TIME CONSTRAINTS
- Core gameplay loop must be functional within 3 days
- Polish features are secondary to functional core
- Documentation required for submission

DC4: ASSET CONSTRAINTS
- Use free Unity Asset Store packages only
- Low-poly art style for performance and rapid development
- No custom 3D modeling required
